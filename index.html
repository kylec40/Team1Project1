Company History

For 40 years, our company has provided the top quality service to those looking to fufill their dreams of becoming an author. From our start as a small bookstore that would help those who have hopes of being a New York Times bestseller to being the fifth largest book publisher in the U.S. We produce approximately 6,000 new books every year in multiple languages.

LOGOs cares deeply about its authors and has agents working around the clock to help those who need help in getting their big break and fufilling their dreams. Our award winning staff has helped authors achieve awards like the Pulizter Price and the Booker Prize among others. We are dedicated to making sure that our authors have the proper help with our deep connections in the book industry to have their dream seen by a large audience.
